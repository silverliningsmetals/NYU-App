# EQ Endorsement — Q3 scaffold (the last credibility blocker)

**Q3 (verbatim):** *"Describe the most important piece of constructive feedback you have given the applicant.
Please detail the circumstances and the applicant's response."* — answered by **Col Johnson**, in his voice.

**Why it's the blocker:** Q3 is the single most credibility-building question in any recommendation. A vague
answer ("Ryan is always eager to learn") signals a coached letter and caps the endorsement at median. A real,
specific feedback-and-change story is what proves coachability + self-management — exactly what Stern's EQ
doctrine rewards. The rest of the packet (Q4 Gen Hadir KLE, Q5 pay-it-forward) is strong; **this is the gap.**

> **Only you can supply the truth here.** Pick the ONE that actually happened, then give me the four details
> below and I'll draft the final paragraph for Col Johnson to rewrite in his voice.
